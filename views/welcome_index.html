Run your own Bank

The best way to protect your digit assets is to split them up into multiple hot wallets on many different severs and treat it like a family business. More than one person is trusted with the private keys to keep the business running in the event of anyone's death. i.e. you don't want all your assets in a major exchange for fear of that entity failing, and you don't want a cold wallet with a password known only by one person, you want a family business that can keep running for generations.
